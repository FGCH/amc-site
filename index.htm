Building Better Bad Banks



Mark Hallerberg & Christopher Gandrud

From the German Federal Archives


We are examining the political economy of public asset management companies ('bad banks'). We are particularly interested in why they are created, how they operate, and how they could be designed more effectively.

We are based at The Fiscal Governance Centre at The Hertie School of Governance and are generously funded by the Deutsche Forschungsgemeinschaft. The project began in summer 2013.
